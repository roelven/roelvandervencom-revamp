After a splendid Music Hack Day London in 2011 we were lucky to be invited to a small tour in the [National Physical Laboratory](http://npl.co.uk/) of London. [Ian Butterworth](http://www.ianbutterworth.co.uk/) was so kind to show us around and illustrate the impressive effects of the acoustic chambers they use for testing. These recordings have been sitting around on my [SoundCloud account](http://soundcloud.com/roelven) for ages and I am finally posting them here for you to enjoy. The quality is not great since I recorded these with an iPad, but you should be able to get a pretty good impression.
