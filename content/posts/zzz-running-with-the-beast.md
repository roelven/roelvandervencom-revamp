Browsing my Spotify playlists I stumbled upon this great album again [zZz - Running with the Beast](http://open.spotify.com/album/2BJQu8ezp18PMGJsvd3A1B). I saw them once years ago as a support act and was really impressed by the energy and performance these guys were putting up. A bit later they released their album Running with the Beast, for which [Roel Wouters aka Xelor](http://roelwouters.com/) has made this awesome video. I agree, it’s pushing quite some moral boundaries but I think that’s mostly because of the switching of contexts, both creative and biological. There is an [animal welfare statement on Roel’s website](http://www.xelor.nl/xelor/media/texts/RwtB_Statement.pdf) to make sure you won’t loose any sleep. If you want to know more, check out [todayandtomorrow](http://www.todayandtomorrow.net/2008/11/24/running-with-the-beast/) for more posters and the ‘making of’ video. This video is 3 years old so no HD quality unfortunately, but still a great watch. Enjoy.
